About the Pizzazone

The Pizzazone was founded year 2020 with the ambition to bring classic italian pizza to Sweden. Our pizza makers use traditional recepties and bake at 400 degrees in stoneoven.

Our priority is always quality, authenticity, taste and good service. We use only the finest ingredients, sourced locally and from Italy, to ensure every pizza is a true culinary experience.
